About

I am an interaction designer interested in how people experience the world — not only through interfaces, but also through attention, time, and perception.

Approach

I believe that good interaction design is not only usability — it is also rhythm, clarity, and meaning. I enjoy working with simple structures, subtle interactions, and interfaces that guide without overwhelming.

Background

I am currently studying design at the University of Aveiro. Beyond digital work, I also explore product design and aesthetic thinking.

Perspective

I am interested in creating calm, intuitive, and intentional interfaces. My work aims to reduce excess and give space to what is essential.
